Family: Hydrocharitaceae

Vallisneria americana

Wild Celery

Conservation Status

Key Characteristics

  • Limp, ribbon-like leaves
  • "Water-surface" pollination method. Female flowers reach the surface on long, tightly coiled stalks. Male flowers break off at the base, float to the surface, and drift until they encounter a female flower.
  • Stabilizes sediments with its root systems, reduces turbidity by slowing water flow, and absorbs excess nutrients like nitrogen and phosphorus from the water column.
